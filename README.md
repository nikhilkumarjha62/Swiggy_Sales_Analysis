# Swiggy Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes 197K+ food delivery records to extract business insights on revenue trends, customer behavior, pricing segments, and geographic performance.

## 📊 KPIs Analyzed

Total Revenue

Average Rating

Average Order Value

Quarterly Growth

Revenue Concentration (Pareto 80/20)

## 🔍 Key Analyses

Monthly & Weekly Revenue Trends

Revenue by State & City

Veg vs Non-Veg Contribution

Category Performance

High Revenue Low Rating Risk Detection

Premium vs Budget Segment Analysis

Correlation between Price & Rating

## 🛠 Tools Used

Python

Pandas

NumPy

Matplotlib

Seaborn

Plotly

## 📈 Dataset Size

197,430 rows
12 columns

## 🚀 Business Impact

Identified revenue concentration across top states

Highlighted premium segment revenue contribution

Detected underperforming restaurants

Evaluated pricing vs customer satisfaction relationship
